Defining Storylines

Saudi Arabia vs Argentina · Group C

The Saudi Shock

Saudi Arabia, ranked 51st, beat eventual champions Argentina 2–1 in the opening round — one of the greatest upsets in World Cup history.

Morocco · Semi-final

Africa's Historic First

Morocco became the first African and first Arab nation to reach a World Cup semi-final, eliminating Belgium, Spain (pens) and Portugal along the way.

Japan · Group E

Japan's Comeback Doubles

Japan beat both Germany and Spain 2–1 from a goal down to top a group of giants — sending Germany out of the group stage for the second WC running.

Final · 18 Dec

The Greatest Final

Mbappé's hat-trick forced a 3–3 draw with Messi-led Argentina. Argentina won the shoot-out 4–2; Mbappé took the Golden Boot with 8 goals, Messi took the trophy.

Belgium · Group F

The #2's Group Exit

FIFA's #2-ranked side crashed out of the group, beaten 2–0 by Morocco and held by Croatia — ending the "golden generation" without a deep run.

Croatia · Bracket

Penalty Specialists Again

Croatia beat Japan and Brazil on penalties to reach a second consecutive medal — Modrić's bronze ball at age 37 capped a remarkable cycle.
